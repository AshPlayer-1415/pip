# Winsy AI Landing Page Copy

## Hero Headline

Meet Winsy AI, a calmer way to remember the small things.

## Subheadline

A local-first macOS assistant companion for reminders, quick storage, and lightweight desktop actions. No account, no cloud sync, and no real AI model or connectors yet.

## Feature Section 1: A Gentle Desktop Companion

Winsy AI lives in your menu bar and keeps a small companion bubble near the corner of your screen. It is present when useful and quiet when not.

## Feature Section 2: Your Day at a Glance

The Today dashboard shows your current mode, next reminder, missed quiet-mode count, and quick actions for Water, Eye Break, Stretch, and Motivate Me.

## Feature Section 3: Local Reminders

Create simple reminders for pills, birthdays, tasks, or anything custom. Winsy AI keeps the flow compact and focused.

## Feature Section 4: Personalities Without Noise

Choose a companion name, avatar style, and personality tone. Winsy AI's messages are warm, short, and designed to feel supportive rather than distracting.

## Feature Section 5: Quiet When You Need It

Snooze nudges for 15 minutes, 1 hour, until tomorrow, or until you turn them back on. Presentation Safe Mode suppresses Winsy AI notifications and bubble popups while you share your screen.

## Privacy-First Section

Winsy AI is local-first. It does not require an account, does not sync to the cloud, does not use a remote database, and does not include a real AI model or connectors yet. Settings, reminders, and Quick Storage files stay on your Mac.

## Known Limitations Note

Winsy AI v0.4.0 is a public beta. Presentation Safe Mode is manual, reminders use simple daily times, and the macOS DMG is not notarized unless Apple Developer credentials are configured. See `KNOWN_LIMITATIONS.md` for details.
