# Pip v0.1.0 Marketing Copy

## One-Line Tagline

A calm, private desktop companion for gentle reminders and better workdays.

## Short Product Description

Pip is a local-first macOS companion that lives in your menu bar, keeps a small bubble near your desktop corner, and helps with reminders, hydration, screen breaks, stretching, and motivation without accounts, cloud sync, or AI dependency.

## GitHub Release Description

Pip v0.1.0 is the first public beta of a gentle macOS desktop companion for reminders and healthy work rhythms.

This release includes a menu bar app, floating companion bubble, first-time onboarding, personality-based avatars and messages, a Today dashboard, local reminders, Private Mode, Presentation Safe Mode, snooze controls, an in-app About section, Reset Pip, and DMG packaging.

Pip is local-first: no account, no cloud sync, no remote database, and no AI service dependency. Reminder data and settings stay on your Mac.

Known beta limitations are documented in `KNOWN_LIMITATIONS.md`.

## Product Hunt Style Description

Pip is a calm macOS desktop companion for people who want reminders without another noisy productivity dashboard. It sits in your menu bar, keeps a small companion bubble nearby, and gently nudges you to drink water, rest your eyes, stretch, stay motivated, or remember small tasks.

No account. No cloud sync. No AI dependency. Just a focused local-first beta for better workdays.

## Reddit Post Draft

Title: I built Pip, a local-first macOS companion for gentle reminders

Hey folks, I built a small macOS app called Pip. It is a desktop companion for reminders, hydration nudges, screen breaks, stretching, and lightweight motivation.

The idea is to make reminders feel calm instead of annoying. Pip lives in the menu bar, shows a small floating bubble, and has a compact Today dashboard with the next reminder, active mode, missed quiet-mode count, and quick actions.

Privacy was a big part of the v0.1 beta:

- no account or login
- no cloud sync
- no remote database
- no AI dependency
- reminders and settings are stored locally on your Mac

It also has Private Mode for generic notification text and Presentation Safe Mode to suppress Pip popups while screen sharing.

This is a v0.1.0 beta, so it is intentionally small and still has limitations. I would love feedback on the product feel: whether the nudges are calm enough, what feels missing, and what would make this worth keeping open every day.
