# Winsy AI v0.4.0 Marketing Copy

## One-Line Tagline

A calm, private desktop companion for gentle reminders and better workdays.

## Short Product Description

Winsy AI is a local-first macOS assistant companion that lives in your menu bar, keeps a small bubble near your desktop corner, and helps with reminders, quick storage, and lightweight desktop actions without accounts or cloud sync.

## GitHub Release Description

Winsy AI v0.4.0 is a rebrand and stability beta for a local-first macOS assistant companion.

This release includes a menu bar app, floating companion bubble, Assistive Touch-style quick menu, Quick Storage shelf, first-time onboarding, local reminders, Private Mode, Presentation Safe Mode, Reset Winsy AI, and DMG packaging.

Winsy AI is local-first: no account, no cloud sync, no remote database, and no real AI model or connector yet. Reminder data, settings, and Quick Storage files stay on your Mac.

Known beta limitations are documented in `KNOWN_LIMITATIONS.md`.

## Reddit Post Draft

Title: I built Winsy AI, a local-first macOS companion for gentle reminders

Hey folks, I built a small macOS app called Winsy AI. It is a local-first assistant companion for reminders, Quick Storage, and lightweight desktop actions.

The idea is to make reminders feel calm instead of annoying. Winsy AI lives in the menu bar, shows a small floating bubble, and has a compact Today dashboard with the next reminder, active mode, missed quiet-mode count, and quick actions.

Privacy is a big part of this beta:

- no account or login
- no cloud sync
- no remote database
- no real AI model or connectors yet
- reminders and settings are stored locally on your Mac

It also has Private Mode for generic notification text and Presentation Safe Mode to suppress Winsy AI popups while screen sharing.

This is a v0.4.0 beta, so it is intentionally small and still has limitations. I would love feedback on the product feel: whether the quick menu feels useful, what feels missing, and what would make this worth keeping open every day.
